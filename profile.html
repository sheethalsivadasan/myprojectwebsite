ABOUT US

Zuhana Exporters Pvt Ltd is a leading garment exporter based in Tirupur, India’s export hub for knitwear of the highest quality. With our state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment, we continually uphold our promise of meeting global standards, and excel in manufacturing a wide range of menswear, womenswear and kidswear.
Founded in 1991, Zuhana Exporters Pvt Ltd has grown from strength to strength, and successfully forged ahead under the able leadership of Mr. PB Navas Khan our Founding Director. Our dedicated staff adheres to ethical practices and ensures consistent quality checks to offer only the best for our clients.
Rooted in professionalism and goodwill, we manufacture clothing of the highest quality at competitive prices.
